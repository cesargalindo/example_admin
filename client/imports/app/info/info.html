Here are some general guidelines to follow in entering item name, size and units information correctly.

1) Use units "ct" if an item is not sold/classified by volume or weight

2) Size equal the total size sold for that pack/box/bag/etc.

3) Highly recommended to perform a Goolge UPC Search in determining optimal Item Name

4) The brand/manfucturer should also be included in the Item Name

5) If you're still unsure about Item Name, Size and Units, we recommend you search exsting Items for similar items matches. Viewing similar items will provide you a better idea on what information to enter.
